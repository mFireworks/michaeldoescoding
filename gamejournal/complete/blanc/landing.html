Blanc

Release Date: February 14th, 2023
Genre: Co-Op Adventure
Developer: Casus Ludi
Platform: Nintendo Switch and PC
Details: Website

Blanc is the first game developed by Casus Ludi, and is a short co-op adventure game of a wolf cub and fawn losing their families in the snow. They begin to travel together in search of their families whereabouts. This is a really short game, and while it doesn't present any real challenge, it's a nice experience to play through with someone. This is a special game to my fiancee and I, as it was the first "date night" co-op game that we played together. Right now, we're both between games and I saw I was missing an achievement in the game, so we did a second playthrough. I don't have much else to say, the game looks fantastic with the hand-drawn graphics, and the sliding section had us giggling the whole time. If you're looking for a quick game to playthrough with a friend or significant other, this might be a good choice, regardless of their proficiency with games.
